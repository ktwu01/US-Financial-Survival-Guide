# 信用卡争议(Chargeback)模板

## 使用场景

- 商品未收到
- 商品与描述不符
- 未授权交易
- 重复扣款
- 商家拒绝退款

---

## 基本信息

**你的信息**:
- 姓名: [全名]
- 账号后四位: [XXXX]
- 交易日期: [日期]
- 交易金额: $[金额]
- 商家名称: [商家]

## 具体情况模板

### 1. 商品未收到
```
I ordered [商品] on [日期] but never received it.

Timeline:
- Order date: [日期]
- Expected delivery: [日期]
- Tracking shows: [状态]
- Contacted merchant: [日期] (no response/refused refund)

I request a full refund of $[金额].
```

### 2. 商品与描述不符
```
The product I received is significantly different from what
was advertised.

Issues:
- Advertised: [描述]
- Received: [实际情况]
- Attempted to return: [日期]
- Merchant response: [拒绝/未回应]

I have attached photos showing the discrepancy.
```

### 3. 未授权交易
```
I did not authorize this charge.

- I did not make this purchase
- My card was not present
- I do not recognize the merchant
- I have not shared my card information

I request immediate reversal of this fraudulent charge.
```

### 4. 重复扣款
```
I was charged multiple times for a single transaction.

- Original charge: $[金额] on [日期]
- Duplicate charge: $[金额] on [日期]
- Contacted merchant: [日期]
- No refund issued

Please reverse the duplicate charge.
```

### 5. 服务未提供
```
I was charged for services that were not provided.

- Service ordered: [描述]
- Payment: $[金额] on [日期]
- Service not delivered as of [日期]
- Contacted merchant: [日期] (no response)

I request a full refund.
```

### 6. 取消订阅仍扣款
```
I canceled my subscription but was still charged.

- Subscription service: [服务名]
- Cancellation date: [日期]
- Confirmation: [确认号/email]
- Still charged: $[金额] on [日期]
- Contacted merchant: No refund issued

Please reverse this charge.
```

## 提交方式

### 在线提交（最快）
- 登录信用卡账户
- 找到交易
- 点击"Dispute"或"Report a Problem"
- 填写在线表格
- 上传文件

### 电话（次选）
- 拨打卡背面电话
- 要求dispute department
- 说明情况
- 要求confirmation number
- 跟进书面文件

### 邮寄（最慢但有记录）
- Certified Mail with Return Receipt
- 保留副本

---

## 时间限制

⚠️ **重要**:
- 必须在账单日期后60天内提出争议
- 越早越好
- 不要等待

---

## 期待结果

### 时间线
- **立即**: 临时credit（Provisional Credit）
- **30-90天**: 银行调查
- **调查后**: 永久credit或恢复扣款

### 可能结果
- ✅ 争议成功: 永久退款
- ❌ 争议失败: 钱退回商家
- ⚠️ 部分成功: 部分退款

---

## 如果争议被拒

1. 要求详细说明理由
2. 提供额外证据
3. 重新提交dispute
4. 考虑CFPB投诉
5. 最后手段: Small claims court

---

## 注意事项

### ✅ Do
- 立即行动
- 提供详细信息
- 保留所有证据
- 诚实描述情况
- 先尝试与商家解决

### ❌ Don't
- 撒谎或夸大
- 滥用dispute权利
- 在dispute期间付款
- 忘记跟进

⚠️ **警告**: 滥用chargeback可能导致账户关闭！
