## 申请信模板

```
[日期]

[医院名称]
Financial Assistance Department
[医院地址]

Re: Charity Care Application
Patient Name: [你的名字]
Account Number: [账号]
Date of Service: [就诊日期]

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to formally apply for financial assistance/charity
care for medical services I received on [日期].

I am an F-1 international student at [学校名] with limited
financial resources. My total medical bill is $[金额], which
I cannot afford to pay.

My current financial situation:
- Status: F-1 Student
- Monthly income: $[金额] (from [来源: 奖学金/工作])
- Monthly expenses: $[金额]
- Savings: $[金额]
- Dependents: [人数]

I have attached the following documents as proof of my financial
situation:
- Bank statements for the past 3 months
- I-20 showing F-1 student status
- [其他文件]

I respectfully request that you review my application for maximum
financial assistance available under your charity care program.

I am willing to provide any additional documentation you may
require. Please contact me at [电话] or [email].

Thank you for your consideration.

Sincerely,
[你的签名]
[你的名字打印]
```

---

## 附件清单

申请时需要附上:
- [ ] 银行账单（最近3个月）
- [ ] I-20复印件
- [ ] 收入证明（工资单/奖学金信）
- [ ] 医疗账单复印件
- [ ] 填写好的charity care申请表（向医院索取）

---

## 寄送方式

**强烈建议**:
- 使用USPS Certified Mail with Return Receipt
- 保留邮寄凭证
- 保留所有文件复印件

**寄送地址**:
查看医疗账单上的Financial Assistance地址

---

## 跟进时间表

- **2周后**: 电话跟进，确认收到
- **4周后**: 如未回复，再次联系
- **6周后**: 书面跟进

---

## 常见问题

**Q: 需要多少收入才符合？**
A: 通常家庭收入低于联邦贫困线200-400%，F-1学生通常符合

**Q: 处理需要多久？**
A: 通常2-6周

**Q: 如果被拒绝怎么办？**
A: 可以申请appeal，或寻求其他援助资源

---

**提示**: 保持礼貌但坚持，很多人第一次被拒后通过appeal成功
